About FlatConnect

FlatConnect is designed specifically for small residential societies still managing with traditional methods like paper diaries, Excel sheets, and manual record-keeping. We provide an affordable, easy-to-use digital platform that doesn't require expensive infrastructure or complex training.

Perfect for societies with 8-200 flats that find platforms like MyGate, NoBroker Hood, or Adda too expensive or complicated. Get all essential features at a fraction of the cost, with simple setup and immediate benefits.
